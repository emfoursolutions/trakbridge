What is TrakBridge?

TrakBridge is a stream management system designed to bridge multiple intelligence and tracking sources with TAK (Team Awareness Kit) systems. Built on a Flask-based architecture with multi-threaded stream processing, TrakBridge consolidates real-time positional data from GPS tracking services, open source intelligence feeds, and various location-based APIs into a unified operational picture.

The platform features an extensible plugin system that enables seamless integration with diverse data sources, automated stream lifecycle management, and encrypted data handling for secure operations. Organizations can leverage TrakBridge to aggregate disparate location information streams—from vehicle tracking systems to OSINT feeds—and seamlessly present them within their TAK environment, enhancing situational awareness and operational coordination across teams and platforms.
